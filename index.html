About AZlagacy

AZlegacy is your Financial Companion. My project aids individuals in managing finances, planning side hustles, and gaining insights into asset management. As a solo developer, I spearheaded all aspects of the project, from conception to execution, over 1 week. AZlegacy caters to a diverse audience, including recent graduates, professionals, and entrepreneurs, striving for financial stability and success.

Growing up in West Africa, surrounded by a landscape where poverty seemed entrenched and opportunities scarce, I found myself drawn to the world of entrepreneurship, assets, and the wisdom found in books about money. It was a region where many struggled to make ends meet, and the concept of building wealth through legitimate means was often overshadowed by the prevalence of dishonest practices. Witnessing the challenges faced by my community, especially the youth who were driven to desperate measures for financial survival, ignited a fire within me to make a difference. I realized that the path to sustainable prosperity lay not in shortcuts or deceit but in empowering individuals with the knowledge and tools to create their own legacies, the right way. This realization fueled my determination to develop AZlegacy, a platform aimed at providing accessible financial education and resources to African people, enabling them to chart a course towards financial independence and success while upholding integrity and ethical principles. Through AZlegacy, I aspire to redefine the narrative of wealth creation in my community, inspiring a new generation of entrepreneurs to build lasting legacies grounded in honesty, diligence, and empowerment.
